NayePankh is a registered NGO under Sub-Section (ii) of Section 80G/12A of the Income Tax Act.

ABOUT OUR NGO

Think global, Act local.

NayePankh Foundation is one of India's biggest student-led non-governmental organisations with a central purpose of fighting deep-rooted inequalities in education, women's dignity, hunger, and emergency relief.

We operate grassroot local chapters across Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and several other northern cities. Our mission is direct, simple, and action-oriented: act local, execute transparently, and make every single rupee countable! Let's revolutionise the society together.

We work tirelessly toward a peaceful, inclusive society where children can thrive to their complete cognitive capabilities and enjoy absolute parity regardless of financial brackets.

By breaking menstrual myths, feeding daily wage workers, putting children into robust formal classrooms, we desire to eliminate foundational disadvantages.

Every donation you secure through NayePankh is eligible for tax refunds with standard 80G certifications.

NGO legitimacy is audited periodically. Your tax bracket will deduct 50% contributions directly from taxable thresholds, giving both corporate donors and individuals an amazing avenue to give back securely.

OUR SOCIAL IMPACT

Our Core Pillars

Active initiatives tackling critical structural needs across marginalized societies in India.

Child Education

Sponsoring school fees, providing textbooks/backpacks, and establishing active weekend learning camps inside slums.

Hygiene Awareness

Defeating taboos by distributing feminine sanitary napkins and spreading scientific puberty health menstrual education.

Hunger Relief

Serving freshly packed nutritious local meals daily to daily wage workers, homeless seniors, and low-wealth households.

Winter & Emergency

Distributing heavy winter blankets, high-density clothing packs, and support resources during extreme seasons or crises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, NayePankh Foundation is a government-registered NGO (Reg No: UP Government). We are dynamic, legally recognized, and completely cleared to collect charitable contributions under the Sub-Section of Section 80G and 12A.

When donating through our online stepping portal, choose to input your income Tax PAN Card Number. Upon approval, NayePankh Foundation compiles and dispatches your official Form 10BE directly to your email, allowing you to deduct 50% of the donated sum from your taxable earnings limits.

We operate under absolute transparency benchmarks! Annual reports detailing resource disbursements are distributed. Over 92% of all capital inflows are dedicated exclusively to direct beneficiary services (books, packets, nutrition, relief goods) and local field logistics rather than administrative overhead.
